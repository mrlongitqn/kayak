This cycle/cruise combo deal is undoubtedly one of the coolest ways to tour Hoi An. Start with a casual cycle through emerald green rice paddies and villages, then board a boat for a sunset island-style barbecue.

FULL ITINERARY

You will be met by your English speaking guide at your hotel in Hoi An at 8am for your cycle and kayak tour. After a brief introduction and biking guidance, you will begin your tour cycling to Hoi An’s countryside. Don’t be intimidated by cycling in Hoi An, the locals have spent their lives on two wheels and though it can all look a bit hectic it is actually a relaxing and amazing experience. You will get a chance to visit a variety of villages and see a whole host of craftsmen at work – from wood carving, sedge mat making, to bamboo making villages. Partake in some local activities and observe our community at play. We will cycle across scenic rice paddy fields and lovely rivers. We will keep at a nice and easy pace, taking in the scenery and local hospitality. After lunch with a local family, you will kayak around the palm islands and learn more about Hoi An’s countryside and river life. The palm forest is a unique mangrove ecosystem for Hoi An and the scenery is spectacular. There is great opportunity to see special fishing traps, fishing boats and observe our community at play. Interact with the fishermen and women who still use traditional fishing methods and who live on the river. Enjoy some light refreshments before returning to your hotel.
